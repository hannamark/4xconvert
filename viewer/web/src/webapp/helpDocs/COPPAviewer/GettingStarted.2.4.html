Logging In to the CTRP Viewer
Once you have created a CTRP Viewer account, and have gained appropriate report access, you can log in to run reports.
How to Log In to the CTRP Viewer
1.
Do one of the following to access the login page:
•
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page, click Log In.
- or -
•
On the banner at the top of the page on the right side, click Log In.
The Login page appears.
2.
In the Login ID and Password fields, type the email address and password you registered earlier. See Creating an Account for more information on creating a user account.
3.
Click Log In.
