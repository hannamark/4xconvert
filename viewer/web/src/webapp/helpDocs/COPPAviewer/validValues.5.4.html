Trial Identifier Types
Valid values for Trial Identifier Types are as follows
•
NCI. National Cancer Institute
•
NCT. National Clinical Trial program (Clinical Trials.gov)
•
CTEP. Clinical Therapy Evaluation Program.
•
DCP. Division of Cancer Prevention
•
Other Identifier. Additional trial identifier such as unique identifier from other registries, NIH grant numbers, or protocol numbers assigned by the Review Board
•
Lead Organization
