Trial Statuses
Valid values for Trial Statuses are as follows:
 
Trial Status
Definition
In Review
Trial currently under IRB review.
Withdrawn
Trial has been withdrawn from development and review prior to enrollment of first patient.
Approved
Trial has been approved, but is not yet recruiting or enrolling patients.
Active
Trial is open for accrual and enrollment.
Enrolling by Invitation
Participants are being (or will be) selected from a predetermined population.
Temporarily Closed to Accrual
Recruiting or enrolling participants has halted prematurely but potentially will resume.
Participants already on trial continue to receive intervention.
Temporarily Closed to Accrual and Intervention
Recruiting or enrolling of participants has halted prematurely but potentially will resume.
Patients are not receiving intervention while recruiting or enrolling patients has been halted.
Closed to Accrual
Trial has been closed to participant accrual, but trial is ongoing (i.e., patients are being treated or examined).
Closed to Accrual and Intervention
Trial has been closed to participant accrual. Participants are not receiving intervention, but continue to be monitored for endpoints such as long term survival.
Administratively Complete
Recruiting or enrolling patients has halted prematurely (for example, due to poor accrual, insufficient drug supply, IND closure, etc.), and will not resume. Participants are no longer being examined or treated.
Complete
Trial has been closed to accrual and follow-up. The participants have completed treatment/intervention, are no longer monitored for trial endpoints (i.e., last patient’s visit has occurred). The trial has met its objectives.
