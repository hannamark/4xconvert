Accessing Trial Summary Reports
Search results returned from ad hoc queries provide access to the Trial Summary Reports (TSRs) for all trials that have reached the processing status Accepted and beyond.
How to Access Trial Summary Reports
1.
Run an Ad Hoc report by following the instructions in Running Ad Hoc Reports.
2.
In the search results table, navigate to the trial of interest, and then click the View TSR hyperlink.
The TSR opens in another window.
