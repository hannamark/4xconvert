About CTRO Reporting
The CTRP Viewer enables you to track the submission, validation, and abstraction of trials via a series of pre-defined report types. You can filter report queries to return statistics that are specific to your needs. For example, you can include or exclude CTEP/DCP-submitted trials in your query.
Many reports allow you to drill down to the milestone and/or trial submission level. Milestones are phases of the trial processing life cycle. They are valuable for tracking the progress of a trial along its processing life cycle. Submission type characterizes a trial as either an original submission or an amendment to an existing trial. A trial can be amended more than once during the trial life cycle.
