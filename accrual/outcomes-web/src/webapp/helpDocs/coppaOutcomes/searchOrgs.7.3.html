Adding Treatment Site Records
The procedure below provides instructions for adding a treatment site record if the one you are searching for is not listed among the registered organizations.
How to Add Organization Records
1.
Navigate to the My Account page.
2.
To the right of the Treatment Site field, click Look Up.
You will not be able to change this field value when updating your account information via the My Account page if you have submitted one or more patient outcome records previously under your current account.
The Select Treatment Site window appears.
3.
Click Add Org.
The Add Organization window appears.
4.
In the Organization Name field, type the full name of your organization.
5.
Provide information in all required fieldsâ€”those marked with an asterisk (*), and then click Save.
The results of your search are displayed in a table below the search criteria fields.
6.
In the Action column of the physician record, click Select.
Your new organization is saved in the system, and the Treatment Site field is populated with the name you just registered.
