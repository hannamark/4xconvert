Creating an Account
Before you begin to use the Outcomes application, create an account using the self-registration feature in the NCI Outcomes Portal.
You must provide, and have access to, a valid email address to create an account.
How to Register as a New User
1.
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page , click Create Account.
The Create Account page appears.
2.
Type a valid email address and password in the fields provided. Passwords must contain a minimum of eight characters and include at least on of the following character types:
•
One numeric character (e.g. 1,2,3)
•
One non alpha-numeric character (for example, !,@, #)
3.
Re-type your password in the field provided.
4.
Click Submit.
A message appears indicating that the system has sent a confirmation email to the email address you provided.
5.
Open the confirmation email and click the embedded link to confirm your registration.
The My Account page appears. The Email Address, Password, and Re-type Password fields are pre-populated with the information you provided.
Contact information is required for internal administrative use only. Your information is not revealed to the public.
6.
Complete the remaining personal information fields. Provide your professional information only. An asterisk (*) beside a field indicates that the information is required.
If the address you provide is outside of the United States, select the State option None (International).
7.
In the PRS Organization Name field, type the name of the organization as it is registered in the ClinicalTrials.gov Protocol Registration System.
8.
To the right of the Treatment Site field, click Look Up, and follow the instructions in Looking Up Treatment Sites to search for registered treatment sites.
9.
To the right of the Physician field, click Look Up, and follow the instructions in Looking Up Treatment Sites to search for registered physicians.
10.
When you have provided all required information, click Submit.
You can change your account information and/or password after you have logged in to Outcomes. To do so, on the navigation pane on the left side of the page, click My Account, and proceed to Step 2 above.
Note:
You can not update Treatment Site and Physician values if you have sub­mitted at least one patient record under your current account.
