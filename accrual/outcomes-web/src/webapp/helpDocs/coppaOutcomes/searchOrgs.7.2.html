Looking Up Treatment Sites
The procedure below provides instructions for searching for a treatment site registered with the Clinical Trials Reporting Program. If you are unable to find your treating site, you can add a new organization record by following the instructions in Adding Treatment Site Records.

1.
Navigate to the My Account page.
2.
To the right of the Treatment Site field, click Look Up.
You will not be able to change this field value when updating your account information via the My Account page if you have submitted one or more patient outcome records previously under this account.
The Select Treatment Site window appears.
3.
Provide as much information as you can about the treatment site. For example, if you know just the city location, type it in the City field.
You must enter search criteria in at least one field.
You can type a series of characters in the Name, City, or Zip fields to narrow the search results. Because the system adds wildcards for you, do not type wildcard symbols in the search fields.
4.
Click Search.
The Search Treatment Site window displays the results of your search.
If your site is not listed, you may have searched too narrowly (that is, you may have provided too much information about the organization). If the list of results is very long and contains many sites that are similar to yours, you can narrow your search by providing more information.
5.
If a treatment site was not listed, broaden your search by deleting one or more of your criteria. For example, if you searched by part of the site’s name, city, state, and zip code in your original search, you may want to search by state alone.
6.
If your site is not listed, follow the instructions in Adding Treatment Site Records. Otherwise, proceed to the next step.
7.
Scroll through the results list until you locate your site, and then click Select.
