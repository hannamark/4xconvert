Updating Patient Records
Outcomes enables you to update patient records that you have submitted previously.
How to Update Patient Records
1.
In the List of Patients, navigate to the patient record that you want to update.
2.
In the Update column, click the Update icon ().
The Update Patient page appears.
3.
Follow Steps 3 through step 10 in Adding New Patient Records.
The patient’s record is updated and you are returned to the List of Patients. The navigation pane expands to display links to update all aspects of the patient’s information.
