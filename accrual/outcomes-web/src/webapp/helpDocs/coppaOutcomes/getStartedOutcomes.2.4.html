Logging In to the NCI Outcomes Portal
Once you have created a NCI Outcomes Portal account, you can log in to search for and submit patient outcome records.
Gather all the data you need before you begin. The system logs you out if it detects that you have not used the application for four hours.
How to Log In to the NCI Outcomes Portal
1.
Navigate to the NCI Outcomes Portal home page at:
http://trials.nci.nih.gov/outcomes
2.
Do one of the following to access the login page:
•
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page, click Login.
- or -
•
On the banner at the top of the page on the right side, click Login.
The Login page appears.
3.
Type the email address and password you registered earlier. See Creating an Account for more information on creating a user account.
If you have forgotten your password, see the instructions in Resetting Your Password.
4.
Click Login.
The Search Patients/List of Patients page appears.
After you have logged in to the NCI Outcomes Portal, you can proceed to search for and/or add patient and clinical outcome information in the system. See Working With Patient Records
