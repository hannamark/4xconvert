Adding Physician Records
The procedure below provides instructions for adding a physician record if the one you are searching for is not listed among the registered physicians.
How to Add Physician Records
1.
Navigate to the My Account page.
2.
To the right of the Treatment Site field, click Look Up.
You will not be able to change this field value when updating your account information via the My Account page if you have submitted one or more patient outcome records previously under this account.
The Select Physician window appears.
3.
Click Add Person.
The Add Person window appears.
4.
Provide information in all required fieldsâ€”those marked with an asterisk (*), and then click Save.
5.
The results of your search are displayed in a table below the search criteria fields.
6.
In the Action column of the physician record, click Select.
Your new physician information is saved in the system, and the Physician field is populated with the name you just registered.
