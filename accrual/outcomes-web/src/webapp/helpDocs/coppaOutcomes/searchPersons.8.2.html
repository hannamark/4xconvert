Looking Up Registered Physicians
The procedure below provides instructions for searching for a physician registered in the Clinical Trials Reporting Program. If you are unable to find your physician, you can add a new physician record by following the instructions in Adding Physician Records.

1.
Navigate to the My Account page.
2.
To the right of the Physician field, click Look Up.
You will not be able to change this field value when updating your account information via the My Account page if you have submitted one or more patient outcome records previously under your current account.
The Select Physician window appears.
3.
Provide as much information as you can about the physician. For example, if you know just the city location, type it in the City field.
You must enter search criteria in at least one field.
You can type a series of characters in the Name, City, or Zip fields to narrow the search results. Because the system adds wildcards for you, do not type wildcard symbols in the search fields.
4.
Click Search.
The Select Physician window displays the results of your search.
If your physician is not listed, you may have searched too narrowly (that is, you may have provided too much information about the physician). If the list of results is very long and contains many names that are similar to yours, you can narrow your search by providing more information.
5.
If a physician was not listed, broaden your search by deleting one or more of your criteria. For example, if you searched by city, state, and zip code in your original search, you may want to search by state alone.
6.
If your physician is still not listed, follow the instructions in Adding Physician Records. Otherwise, proceed to the next step.
7.
Scroll through the results list until you locate your physician, and then click Select.
