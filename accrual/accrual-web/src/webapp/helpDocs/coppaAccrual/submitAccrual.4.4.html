Completing Accrual Submissions
For each trial listed, the Accrual Submissions page displays submitted and open accrual submissions that exist for the trial to date. You can complete the opened accrual submission records only.
When you complete a submission, the system changes the associated study subjects’ status from “Pending” to “Active”.
How to Complete Opened Accrual Records
1.
Navigate to the appropriate trial and click the NCI Trial Identifier. For details, see Searching for Trials.
The Accrual Submissions page appears.
2.
In the Status column, locate the open record, and click the Submit icon.
3.
On the pop-up window, click OK to confirm your submission.
The Accrual Submissions page displays your record. The submission date and your user name appear along with the record, and the status is changed to “Submitted”.
