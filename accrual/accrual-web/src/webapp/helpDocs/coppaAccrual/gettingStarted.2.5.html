Updating Current CTRP Accrual Accounts
Application support staff will create caGrid account usernames and random passwords for all current CTRP Accrual users who do not have caGrid or NIH accounts.
Application support staff will email this new account information to you automatically; there is no need for you to contact them.
How to Update Your Current CTRP AccrualAccount
1.
Navigate to the CTRP Accrual home page at http://trials.nci.nih.gov/accrual.
2.
Log in using the account name and password that you received from the Application support staff. See Logging In to the CTRP Accrual Site.
3.
On the navigation pane, click My Account.
The My Account page appears, pre-populated with information from your original account, but with a new caGrid username.
4.
Complete the rest of the fields by following the instructions in Creating New CTRP Accrual Accounts, beginning with Step 6.
