Searching for Study Subjects
You can search for a particular study subject record using any combination of the following four criteria:
•
Study Subject ID
•
Participating Site
•
Record Status
•
Birth Date
You must select a trial before you can search for study subjects.
How to Search for Study Subjects
5.
Navigate to the trial you want to work with by following the instructions in Selecting Listed Trials or Searching for Trials, and then click the corresponding NCI Trial Identifier link.
6.
On the Navigation pane, click Study Subject Search.
The Search Study Subject page appears.
7.
Type or select your search criteria from one or more of the fields provided.
You can search for a trial by one or more criteria, or you can list all subjects associated with trials to which you have been granted access by leaving all of the search criteria fields blank.
8.
Click Search.
Your search results are displayed in the List of Study Subject section of the page. The list is sorted by Study Subject ID.
