Logging In to the CTRP Accrual Site
Once you have created a CTRP Accrual account, you can log in to search for clinical trials and submit accrual data.
Gather all the protocol data you need before you begin. The system locks you out if it detects that you have not used the application for 90 minutes. The system also locks you out after three unsuccessful attempts to log in within 24 hours. In the event that you cannot remember your password, or have been locked out of your account, contact Application Support. Contact information is available at http://ncicb.nci.nih.gov/support.
How to Log In to the CTRP Accrual Site
1.
Navigate to the CTRP Accrual home page at http://trials.nci.nih.gov/accrual.
The NCI CTRP Accrual Site Home page presents options for working with the Accrual site.
2.
On the Home page, click the Log In link.
You can also display the Login page by clicking Log In on the navigation pane on the left side of the page or on the banner at the top of the page on the right side.
The Login page appears.
3.
Type your username and password in the fields provided. If you do not have a registered username and password, see Requesting an Accrual Site Account.
If you have forgotten your password, see the instructions in Resetting Your Account.
