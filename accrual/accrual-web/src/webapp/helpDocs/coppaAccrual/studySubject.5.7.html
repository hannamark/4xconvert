Deleting Study Subject Records
You can delete study subjects associated with open accrual records only.
How to Delete Study Subject Records
1.
Navigate to the trial you want to work with by following the instructions in Selecting Listed Trials or Searching for Trials, and then click the corresponding NCI Trial Identifier link.
2.
On the Navigation pane, click Study Subject Search.
The study subject record appears in the List of Study Subjects.
3.
In the list of study sebjects locate the appropriate study subject record, and click the Delete icon.
4.
In the pop-up window, click OK to remove the subject from the study.
