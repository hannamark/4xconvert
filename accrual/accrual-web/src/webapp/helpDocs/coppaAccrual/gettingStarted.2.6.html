Creating New CTRP Accrual Accounts Using caGrid or NCI Credentials
You can create a CTRP Accrual account using your caGrid or NIH credentials.
Although you already have a caGrid or NIH account, the system needs to know the source of your account so that it can verify your identity. Once verified, you can register for an associated CTRP Accrual account.
If you have a caGrid account, your account source is caBIG. If you have an NIH/NCI account, your account source is National Cancer Institute.
How to Create New CTRP Accrual Account Using caGrid or NIH Credentials
1.
Navigate to the CTRP Accrual home page at http://trials.nci.nih.gov/accrual.
2.
Click Create an account.
The Create Account page appears.
3.
Because you already have an NIH or caGrid account, on the Create Account page, click the here link
The Register page appears.
4.
In the Username field, type your caGrid or NIH username.
5.
In the Password field, type your associated password.
6.
Select one of the following account authentication methods from the Account Source drop-down list:
•
If you provided your caGrid account credentials, select caBIG.
•
If you provided your NCI account credentials, select National Cancer Institute.
7.
Click Submit.
The My Account page appears, pre-populated automatically with all the information associated with your caGrid or NCI account.
8.
In the Your Account Profile section, complete the rest of the fields by following the instructions in Creating New CTRP Accrual Accounts, beginning with Step 8.
You can use the NCI Password Station to change your NCI password. The Password Station is available on line at https://password.nci.nih.gov/AIMS/PS/default.aspx.
For more information about changing your password, see Changing Your Password.
