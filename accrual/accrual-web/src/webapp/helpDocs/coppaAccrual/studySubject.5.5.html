Updating Study Subject Records
You can update study subject records only if an opened accrual submission exists.
How to Update Study Subject Records
1.
Navigate to the trial you want to work with by following the instructions in Selecting Listed Trials or Searching for Trials, and then click the corresponding NCI Trial Identifier link.
2.
On the Navigation pane, click Study Subject Search.
3.
The study subject record appears in the List of Study Subjects.In the list of study sebjects, click the Update icon ().
