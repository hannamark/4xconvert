Self-Registering for an Account
You can self-register an account via the NCI CTRP Registration Site.
You must provide, and have access to, a valid email address to create an account.
How to Register an Account in the CTRP Registration Site
1.
Navigate to the CTRP Accrual site at http://trials.nci.nih.gov/accrual, and click Log in.
2.
At the end of the login message above the email address field, click the create an account link.
3.
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page, click Create Account.
The CTRP Registration Site Create Account page appears.
4.
Type a valid email address and password in the fields provided
Passwords are case sensitive.
5.
Re-type your password in the field provided.
6.
Click Submit.
A message appears indicating that the system has sent a confirmation email to the address you provided.
7.
Open the confirmation email and click the embedded link to confirm your registration.
The My Account page appears. The Email Address, Password, and Re-type Password fields are pre-populated with the information you provided.
Note:
Contact information is required for internal administrative use only. Your information is not revealed to the public.
8.
Complete the remaining personal information fields. Provide your professional information only. An asterisk (*) beside a field indicates that the information is required.
Note:
If the address you provide is outside of the United States, select the State option None (International).
9.
In the Organization Affiliation field, type the name of the organization you are affiliated with.
10.
In the PRS Organization Name field, type the name of the organization as it is registered in the ClinicalTrials.gov Protocol Registration System.
The PRS organization name is required for uploading trial records to ClinicalTrials.gov via a system-generated .xml file. The PRS organization name you include in your profile is included in that .xml file. This precludes having to update the PRS name in the .xml file. Therefore it is very important for you to update your account whenever there is a change in PRS.
11.
Click Submit.
