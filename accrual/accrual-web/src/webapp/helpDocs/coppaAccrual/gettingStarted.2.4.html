Creating New CTRP Accrual Accounts
If you are new to NCI Clinical Trials Reporting Program Accrual and you do not have a caGrid or NCI account, request aCTRP Accrual account via the CTRP Registration account creation feature.
How to Create a New CTRP Accrual Account
1.
Navigate to the CTRP Accrual home page at http://trials.nci.nih.gov/accrual.
The Login page appears.
2.
At the end of the login message click the here link.
The NCI CTRP Registration site Create Account page appears.
3.
In the Email Address field, type the email address you will use for submitting accrual data (this should be your business email address), and then click Submit.
The system sends you a link to activate your account.
4.
Open the confirmation email and click the embedded link to the My Account page.
The My Account page appears. The email field is pre-populated with the email address you provided
5.
In the Username field, type the username you would like to use with this account.
6.
In the Password field, type a new password for your account. Passwords must have the following characteristics:
•
A minimum of 10, and fewer than 20 characters
•
At least one upper case letter, one lower case letter, one digit, and one non-alphanumeric symbol
•
No dictionary words
You can use the account you create here for all other caGrid-enabled applications.
7.
Type the same password in the Re-type password field.
8.
In the Your Account Profile section, complete the remaining personal information fields. Provide your professional information only. An asterisk (*) beside a field indicates that the information is required.
Contact information is required for internal administrative use only. Your information is not revealed to the public.
9.
Next to the Organization Affiliation field, click Look Up and select the appropriate organization from the list of search results.
10.
In the PRS Organization Name field, type the full name of the organization as it appears in the ClinicalTrials.gov Protocol Registration System account list at http://prsinfo.clinicaltrials.gov/prs_organizations.html.
11.
Click Submit.
The system sends you an account confirmation email message.
