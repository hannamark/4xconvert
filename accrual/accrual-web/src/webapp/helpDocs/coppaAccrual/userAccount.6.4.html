Resetting Your Account
You can reset your account in the event that you cannot remember your password, or have been locked out of your account. The process for doing so depends on your account source, either caGrid or NIH. The login page has links that direct you to the reset process for each.
How to Reset your password
1.
Navigate to the CTRP Registration Site home page at http://trials.nci.nih.gov/registration.
2.
Click Login.
The Login page appears.
3.
Click the Forgot Your Password? link.
The Reset Password page appears
4.
Do one of the following to reset your password:
•
If you have a caBIG account, click the NCICB Application Support link (http://ncicb.nci.nih.gov/support) and request a password reset.
- or-
•
If you have an NIH account, click the Password Station link (http://password.nci.nih.gov). Once you have logged into the NCI Password Station, you can unlock your account or retrieve your password by answering your private questions. You can also request a password reset from NCICB Application Support.
