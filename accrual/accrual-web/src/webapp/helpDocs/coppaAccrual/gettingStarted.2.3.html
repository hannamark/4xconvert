Requesting an Accrual Site Account
In order to submit accrual records via CTRP Accrual, you must request access from the CTRO (Clinical Trials Reporting Office) and have a valid CTRP Accrual account.
How to Request an Accrual Site Account
1.
Self-register for an account in the NCI CTRP Registration Site by following the instructions in Creating New CTRP Accrual Accounts.
2.
Contact the CTRO to request an account for the Accrual site.You can contact the CTRO via email at ncictrp@mail.nih.gov (mailto:ncictrp@mail.nih.gov).
You can register for an account via the NCI CTRP Registration Site. The CTRP Accrual site home page provides a link to the Registration Site account page.
The way in which you create an account in the CTRP depends on whether you have a current NIH or caGrid account, and whether you are a current or new CTRP Accrual or Registration Site user, as follows:
•
New users who do not have a caGrid or NIH account request caGrid credentials via the CTRP Registration site.
•
Current users who do not have a caGrid or NIH account receive a username and temporary password for a caGrid account.
•
New users who have a caGrid or NIH account use their current credentials to register for an account via the CTRP Registration site.
