Creating an Account
Before you begin to use the Outcomes application, create an account using the self-registration feature in the NCI Outcomes Portal.
You must provide, and have access to, a valid email address to create an account.
How to Register as a New User
1.
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page, click Create Account.
The Create Account page appears.
2.
Type a valid email address and password in the fields provided. Passwords must contain a minimum of the following characters:
•
Six characters
•
One numeric character (e.g. 1,2,3)
3.
Re-type your password in the field provided.
4.
Click Submit.
A message appears indicating that the system has sent a confirmation email to the email address you provided.
5.
Open the confirmation email and click the embedded link to confirm your registration.
The My Account page appears. The Email Address, Password, and Re-type Password fields are pre-populated with the information you provided.
Contact information is required for internal administrative use only. Your information is not revealed to the public.
6.
Complete the remaining personal information fields. Provide your professional information only. An asterisk (*) beside a field indicates that the information is required.
If the address you provide is outside of the United States, select the State option None (International).
7.
In the PRS Organization Name field, type the name of the organization as it is registered in the ClinicalTrials.gov Protocol Registration System.
8.
To the right of the Organization Affiliation field, click Look Up.
The Select Treatment Site window appears.
9.
Provide as much information as you can about the treatment site. For example, if you know just the city location, type it in the City field.
You must enter search criteria in at least one field.
You can type a series of characters in the Name, City, or Zip fields to narrow the search results. Because the system adds wildcards for you, do not type wildcard symbols in the search fields.
10.
Click Search.
The Search Treatment Site window displays the results of your search.
If your site is not listed, you may have searched too narrowly (that is, you may have provided too much information about the organization). If the list of results is very long and contains many sites that are similar to yours, you can narrow your search by providing more information.
11.
If treatment site was not listed, do one of the following to modify your search:
•
To broaden your search so that more sites are listed in the search results, delete one or more of your criteria. For example, if you searched by part of the site’s name, city, state, and zip code in your original search, you may want to search by state alone.
- or -
•
To narrow your search so that fewer sites are listed in the search results, provide more information about your organization. For example, if you searched by site name only in your original search, you may want to search by city in addition to the name.
12.
Scroll through the results list until you locate your site, and then click Select.
13.
To the right of the Physician field, click Look Up.
The Select Physician window appears.
14.
Follow Steps 7 through 12 above, substituting physician information for PRS information.
15.
Click Submit.
