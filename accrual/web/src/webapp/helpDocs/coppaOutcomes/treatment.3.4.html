Adding Treatment Regimens
A patient may undergo many treatments during the course of a trial. For each regimen, provide the name/label of the treatment regimen and describe details, e.g., intervention name, schedule, dosage, etc.
Treatment plan names you may have added previously are listed when you click the Treatment Regimen Name field.
How to Add Treatment Regimens
1.
On the navigation pane, under Treatment, click Treatment Regimen.
The Treatment Plan page appears.
2.
Click Add.
The Add Treatment Plan page appears.
3.
In the Treatment Regimen Name field, type the name of the regimen.
If a treatment regimen record already exists, the treatment name may be listed when you click the Treatment Regimen Name field. You can select the name rather than type it.
4.
In the Treatment Regimen Description field, type a description of the regimen. For example, you can include details such as intervention name, schedule, dosage, etc.
5.
Click Save.
