Deleting Patient Records
Outcomes enables you to delete patient records that you have submitted previously. Once you have deleted a patient record, it will no longer be available for query or update.
How to Delete Patient Records
1.
In the List of Patients, navigate to the patient record that you want to delete.
2.
In the Delete column, click the Delete icon ().
The patient record is deleted permanently.
