Updating Patient Records
Outcomes data (e.g., measurement of tumor that enables the treating clinician to make a formal assessment of response, such as Complete Response, Partial Response, Stable Disease, Partial Disease) are typically obtained periodically during treatment (e.g., at the end of a pre-specified number of courses). If a treatment that includes multiple disease assessments, this will require updating the existing patient's outcomes data in the data repository.
