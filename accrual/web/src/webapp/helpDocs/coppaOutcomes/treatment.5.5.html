Updating Treatment Regimen Records
The NCI Outcomes Portal enables you to edit/update existing records.
How to Update Treatment Regimen Records
1.
On the navigation pane, under Treatment, click Treatment Regimen.
The Treatment Regimens page appears.
2.
In the Edit column for the appropriate record, click the Edit icon () and follow Steps 3 through 5 in Submitting Treatment Regimen Data.
