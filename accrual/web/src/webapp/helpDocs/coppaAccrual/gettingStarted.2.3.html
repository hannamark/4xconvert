Requesting an Accrual Site Account
 
In order to submit accrual records via the CTRP Accrual site, you must request access from the (Clinical Trials Reporting Office) CTRO.
How to Request an Accrual Site Account
1.
Self-register for an account in the NCI CTRP Registration Site by following the instructions in Self-Registering for an Account.
2.
Contact the CTRO to request an account for the Accrual site.You can contact the CTRO via email at ncictrp@mail.nih.gov (mailto:ncictrp@mail.nih.gov).
The CTRO verifies your credentials and creates your account using the information you provided when you self-registered via the NCI CTRP Registration Site.
