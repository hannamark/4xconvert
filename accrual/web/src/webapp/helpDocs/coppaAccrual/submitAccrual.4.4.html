Completing Accrual Submissions
For each trial listed, the Accrual Submissions page displays any accruals that may have been submitted previously, and any accruals that are currently open, but not submitted. You can complete the opened accrual records only.
When you complete a submission, the system changes the study subjectsâ€™ status from Pending to Active.
. See patient's record status transition diagram. Submission cannot be completed if at least one patient's record status remains 'pending' on the trial.
 
How to Complete Submitted Accrual Records
1.
Navigate to the appropriate trial and click the NCI Trial Identifier. For details, see Searching for Trials on page 16.
The Accrual Submissions page appears.
2.
In the Status column, locate the open record, and click the Submit icon ().
3.
On the pop-up window, click OK to confirm your submission.
The Accrual Submissions page displays your record. The submission date and your user name appear along with the record, and the status is changed to Submitted.
