Reviewing Study Subject Records
CTRP Accrual enables you to review the collection of de-identified study subject data associated with a particular trial/study registered with the CTRP.
Each study subject record is identified by either of the following statuses:
•
Opened. Study subject records that have not been completed are considered opened. You can create, update, and delete study subject records for accrual records in this state.
•
Submitted. Study subject records that have been completed are considered submitted. You can not modify study subject data associated with submitted accrual records.
How to Review Study Subject Records
1.
Navigate to the appropriate trial and click the NCI Trial Identifier. For instructions on locating a particular trial, see Searching for Trials.
The Accrual Submissions page appears and displays any accrual records that may have been submitted previously, and/or opened but not submitted. Each trial can have only one open accrual record.
2.
On the Navigation pane, click Study Subject Search.
All subjects currently associated with the trial are displayed in the List of Study Subjects section. .
3.
Locate the subject record you want to review, and click the appropriate study subject ID link in the Study Subject ID column.
The View Study Subject page displays the current study subject information .
4.
To return to the study subject record, click Back.
