Reviewing Submitted Accrual Records
Collection of de-identified participant level data, with a focus on demographic information, that are associated with a particular trial/study by organizations providing information to the NCI Clinical Trials Reporting Office (CTRO).
How to Review Submitted Accrual Records
1.
Navigate to the appropriate trial and click the NCI Trial Identifier. For details, see Searching for Trials on page 16.
The Accrual Submissions page appears and displays any accruals that may have been submitted previously, and any accruals that have recently been opened but not submitted.
2.
Locate the accrual record you want to review, and click the accrual title in the Label column.
The Accrual Submission Details page displays the information provided .
3.
To return to the submission, click Back.
