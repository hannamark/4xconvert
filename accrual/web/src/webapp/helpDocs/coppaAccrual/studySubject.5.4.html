Updating Study Subject Records
 
 
How to Update Study Subject Records
1.
Navigate to the trial you want to work with by following the instructions in Selecting Listed Trials on page 15 or Searching for Trials on page 16, and then click the corresponding NCI Trial Identifier link.
2.
On the Navigation pane, click Study Subject Search.
The study subject record appears in the List of Study Subjects.
3.
In the list of study sebjects, click the Update icon ().
4.
The Edit page displays the study subject’s current information.
5.
Make changes as appropriate, and then click Save.
