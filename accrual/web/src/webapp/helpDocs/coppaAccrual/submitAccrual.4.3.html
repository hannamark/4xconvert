How to Submit New Accrual Records
1.
Navigate to the appropriate trial and click the NCI Trial Identifier. For details, see Searching for Trials on page 16.
The Accrual Submissions page appears and displays any accruals that may have been submitted previously.
2.
Click Add New.
The New Accrual Submission Page appears.
3.
In the Submission Title field, type an appropriate title for the accrual record. This title will appear in the Label column of the accrual record.
4.
Do one of the following to record the submission cutoff date:
•
In the Submission Cut-off Date field type the date in the format mm/dd/yyyy.
- or -
•
Click the calendar icon () and select the date from the pop-up calendar.
When submitting the accrual, the cut-off date must be the current date or some time in the past. 9. New Cut-off Date (for the newly created submission) must be same/bigger than the Cut-off-Date of the previous submission for the same trial.1
5.
In the Description field, type a description of the accrual information you are submitting.
6.
Click Save.
The Accrual Submissions page displays the record you just created.
7.
Optionally, to preview the information you want to submit, click the link in the Label column.
The Accrual Submission Details page displays the information you provided.
8.
To continue with the submission, click Back.
9.
On the Accrual Submissions page, click the Submit icon ().
10.
On the pop-up window, click OK to confirm your submission.
The Accrual Submissions page displays your new record. The submission date and your user name appear along with the record, and the status is changed to Submitted.
11.
Optionally, to create another record, click Add New, and repeat the steps above.
