Editing Amendment Details
You can edit your amended trial details after you have reviewed them at any time before you submit the trial to the CTRP.
How to Edit Amendment Details
1.
Scroll to the bottom of the Review Trial Details page, and click Edit.
The Amendment Trial page displays all information you have provided in editable form.
You must upload the amendment-related trial documents again each time you click the Edit button.
2.
Make changes as necessary and then follow the instructions in Reviewing and Submitting Amended Trials.
