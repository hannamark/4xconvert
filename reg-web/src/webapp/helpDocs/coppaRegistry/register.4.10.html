Completing the Trial Related Documents Section
You must include a protocol document, IRB approval document, Informed Consent, and a participating sites document (if applicable) for complete submission. If the Informed Consent and Participating Sites documents are not included as part of the protocol document, upload them separately as “Trial Related Documents.”
The procedure for uploading documents is the same for all document types.
How to Submit Trial Related Documents:
1.
Click the Browse button beside the Protocol Document field.
2.
Navigate to, and select, the appropriate document, and then click Open.
Note:
Depending on your operating system, you may see a different command name for “Open.”
3.
Repeat these steps above for each type of document.
4.
Optionally, to add more documents, click Add New Document and repeat the steps above.
5.
When you have completed all fields, click Submit Trial Details
The Trial Details page appears. It contains a message that you have created your trial successfully (if appropriate), and lists the details of the trial you just submitted. The message prompts you to complete required fields in the event you neglected to do so.
6.
Optionally, to view the trial related documents, click the document link.
7.
Optionally, to add another trial, click the Add Another Trial link located on the top right corner of the details pane.
