Adding Principal Investigators
If your trial’s principal investigator’s name is not currently registered in the system, you can register it at the same time you register your trial. Be sure to search the system’s registered names first before you register a new one. This will ensure that you do not create a duplicate record in the system.
How to Register a Principal Investigator:
1.
On the navigation pane, click Add a Trial, and then navigate to the Lead Organization/Principle Investigator section.
 
2.
Next to the Principle Investigator field, click Look Up.
The Principle Investigator Lookup page appears.
3.
Click Enter New Investigator.
The Enter New Investigator section appears.
4.
Click Enter and Continue.
5.
Your new investigator is saved in the system and the Principal Investigator field on the Add a Trial page is populated with the name you just registered.
