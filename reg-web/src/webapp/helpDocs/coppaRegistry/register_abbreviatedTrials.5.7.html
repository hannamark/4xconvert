Recording Trial-Related Documents
You must include the Protocol Template document if you did not record an NCT number in the Trial Identification section. You can upload other documents as needed.
CTRO uses the ClinicalTrials.gov record to validate submission.
Currently you are required to supply your documents as Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx, or .docm), Adobe PDF, Microsoft Excel (.xls, .xlsx, .xlsm, or .xlsb), and/or WordPerfect files.
Documents you upload here will not be saved if you save this trial as a draft. Only upload the documents when you are ready to submit the trial.
The procedure for uploading documents is the same for all document types.
How to Submit Trial-Related Documents
1.
Next to the Abbreviated Trial Template field, click the Browse button.
2.
Navigate to, and select, the appropriate document, and then click Open.
Depending on your operating system, you may see a different command name for “Open.”
3.
Optionally, repeat the step above to upload another trial-related document.
