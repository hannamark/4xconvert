Displaying and Removing Trial Ownership
As a site administrator, you can assign and revoke trial ownership for any trial owned by your site when your organization is playing the lead organization role. Trial ownership allows a user to view trial details, update trials, and amend complete trials.
TSR and XML documents are sent to all trial owners.
How to Display Trial Ownerhip
1.
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page, click Display Trial Ownership.
The Display Trial Ownership page displays the current owner(s) of the trial you have selected to administer.
2.
To display all the trials owned by a given user in your organization, search for the user by first name, last name, or email address, and then click Search.
All trials owned by the user are listed in the Search Results list.
3.
To display all owners of a given trial in your organization, enter the Trial Identifier in the field provided, and then click Search.
All owners of the selected trial are listed in the Search Results list.
4.
To revoke ownership of a trial, locate the user in the Search Results, and then click Remove.
