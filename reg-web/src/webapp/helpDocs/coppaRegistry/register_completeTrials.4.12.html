Recording Trial Related Documents
You must include each of the following types of documents in order to register your trial:
•
Complete protocol document
No documents are required if an NCT number is provided for complete trials. CTRO uses the ClinicalTrials.gov record to validate submission.
•
IRB approval document
•
Participating sites (if not included in the protocol document). Multi-site trials require a list of participating sites and contact information.
If the Informed Consent and/or Participating Sites are not included as part of the protocol document, upload them separately as “Trial Related Documents.”
•
Informed Consent (if not included in the protocol document)
Currently you are required to supply your documents as Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx, or .docm), Adobe PDF, Microsoft Excel (.xls, .xlsx, .xlsm, or .xlsb), and/or WordPerfect files.
Documents you upload here will not be saved if you save this trial as a draft. Only upload the documents when you are ready to submit the trial.
The procedure for uploading documents is the same for all document types.
How to Submit Trial Related Documents
1.
Next to the Protocol Document field, click the Browse button.
2.
Navigate to, and select, the appropriate document, and then click Open.
Depending on your operating system, you may see a different command name for “Open.”
3.
Repeat these steps above for each type of document.
