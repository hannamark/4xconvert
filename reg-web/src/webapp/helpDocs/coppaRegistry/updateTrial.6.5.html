Editing Updated Details
You can edit your updated trial details after you have reviewed them at any time before you submit them to the CTRP.
How to Edit Updated Details
1.
Scroll to the bottom of the Review Trial Details page, and click Edit.
The Update Trial page displays all information you have provided in editable form.
Ensure that you upload the updated trial-related documents again each time you click the Edit button.
2.
Make changes as necessary and then follow the instructions in Reviewing and Submitting Trial Updates.
