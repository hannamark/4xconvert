Viewing Trial-Related Documents
You can download, and open or save, any documents that have been submitted with a trial. Whether or not you can view a trial document depends on the trial status and who submitted the trial details, as follows:
•
As a trial submitter, you can view your trials that have the status “Submitted” as well as others’ trials that have not been rejected during the validation process.
•
Other registered users can view only trials that do not have a status of “Submitted” or “Rejected.”
How to Download Trial-Related Documents:
1.
Click the NCI Identifier hypertext link associated with the trial of interest.
The metadata for the selected trial is displayed in a new page.
2.
In the Trial Related Documents section at the bottom of the page, click hypertext link associated with the document of interest.
A dialog box appears in which you are given the option to open the document or save it to location of your choice.
3.
Follow the instructions for your browser and operating system to view or save the document.
