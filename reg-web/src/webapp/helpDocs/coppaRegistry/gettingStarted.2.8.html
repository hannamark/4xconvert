Logging In to the CTRP Registration Site
Once you have created a CTRP Registration Site account, you can log in to search for and submit clinical trial details.
Gather all the protocol data you need before you begin. The system locks you out if it detects that you have not used the application for 90 minutes. The system also locks you out after three unsuccessful attempts to log in within 24 hours. In the event that you cannot remember your password, or have been locked out of your account, contact Application Support. Contact information is available at http://ncicb.nci.nih.gov/support.
