Recording Summary 4 Information
Summary 4 categorizes the external sponsor or funding source based on the role/responsibility/participation in the study. It is based on authorship, drug supplement, trial monitoring design, and implementation.
If the lead organization or at least one participating site is an NCI-designated cancer center, complete all fields in the Summary 4 Information section. Fields in this section are optional. The Trial Submission Category Type is pre-populated.
