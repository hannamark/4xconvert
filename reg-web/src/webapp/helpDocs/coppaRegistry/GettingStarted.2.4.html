Logging In To the NCI Clinical Trials Reporting Portal
Once you have registered as a NCI Clinical Trials Reporting Portal submitter, you can enter your user credentials to manage trials in the caBIG repository.
The system locks users out of the portal when there have been 3 unsuccessful attempts to log in within 24 hours. If you are locked out of the system contact the Help Desk. (See Application Support )
How to log in to the NCI Clinical Trials Reporting Portal:
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page, click Log In.
