Logging In to the CTRP Registration Site
Once you have created a CTRP Registration Site account, you can log in to search for and submit clinical trial details.
Gather all the protocol data you need before you begin. The system logs you out if it detects that you have not used the application for 90 minutes.
How to Log In to the CTRP Registration Site
1.
Do one of the following to access the login page:
•
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page, click Login.
- or -
•
On the banner at the top of the page on the right side, click Login.
The Login page appears.
2.
Type the email address and password you registered earlier. See Creating an Account for more information on creating a user account.
If you have forgotten your password, see the instructions in Resetting Your Password.
3.
Click Login.
The Disclaimer page appears.
4.
After you read the information, click Accept.
The Search Trials page appears.
After you have logged in to CTRP Registration Site, you can proceed to search for and/or add clinical trials in the system.
