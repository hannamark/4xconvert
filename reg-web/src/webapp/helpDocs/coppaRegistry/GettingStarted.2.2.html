About the CTRP Registration Site
The CTRP Registration Site provides researchers with access to cancer clinical trials. It enables users to search for clinical trials submitted by members of the cancer research community and to view details of existing trials. Additionally, users who create an account with the CTRP can submit new clinical trial protocol details and amend those currently registered and verified in the CTRP.
The CTRP Registration Site enables users to register trials one-at-a-time, or in batches consisting of multiple trials. For information on registering single trials, see Chapter 3, Registering New Trials,. For information on registering multiple trials, see Registering Multiple Trials in a Batch.
Currently you can register interventional trials. Future releases of this produce will enable you to register observational trials as well.
CTRP Registration Site supports Internet Explorer 7 (IE 7.0.5730.13 and 7.0.5730.11), IE 8 (8.0.6001.18702), and FireFox 3 (3.0.10) browsers.
