Printing Amended and Updated Trial Information
You can print a copy of the amended/updated trial to facilitate the review and/or keep for your records. You must review the amendment in order to access the print feature. For instructions on reviewing the trial, see Reviewing and Submitting Trial Amendments and Updates.
How to Print Amended and Updated Trial Information
1.
Review the amended trial.
2.
Scroll to the bottom of the Review Trial Details page, and click Print.
