Registering Organizations
You can register an organization if you are unable to find your organization listed in the system. Before you register an organization, be sure to search the system’s registered organizations to ensure that you do not create a duplicate record. (See Searching for Registered Organizations ) If your search results do not contain the name of your organization, you can register a new one. (See Adding Organizations )
