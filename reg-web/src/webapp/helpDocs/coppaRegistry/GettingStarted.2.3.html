Registering as a New User
Registering as a NCI Clinical Trial Portal user enables you to search for and submit clinical trial protocol details.
Note:
You must provide, and have access to, a valid e-mail address to register for an account.
How to Register as a New User:
1.
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page , click Register.
The Register page appears.
2.
Type a valid e-mail address and password in the fields provided. Passwords must contain a minimum of the following characters:
•
Six characters
•
One numeric character (e.g. 1,2,3)
3.
Re-type your password in the field provided.
4.
Click Continue.
A message appears indicating that the system has sent a confirmation e-mail to the e-mail address you provided.
5.
Open the confirmation e-mail and click the embedded link to confirm your registration.
The My Account page appears. The E-mail Address, Password, and Re-type Password fields are pre-populated with the information you provided.
6.
Complete the remaining personal information fields. An asterisk (*) beside a field indicates that the information is required.
Note:
If the address you provide is outside of the United States, select the zip code “None.”
7.
In the Affiliate Organization field, type the name of the organization you are affiliated with.
8.
Click Submit.
