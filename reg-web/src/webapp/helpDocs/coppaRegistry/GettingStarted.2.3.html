Registering as a New User
Registering as a NCI Clinical Trial Portal user enables you to search for and submit trial protocol details.
How to Register as a New User:
1.
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page, click Register.
The Register page appears.
2.
Type a valid email address and password in the fields provided. Passwords must contain a minimum of the following characters:
•
Six characters
•
One numeric character (e.g. 1,2,3,)
•
One non alpha-numeric character (e.g. !,@, #)
3.
Re-type your password in the field provided.
4.
Click Continue.
A message appears indicating that the system has sent a confirmation email to the email address you provided.
5.
Open the confirmation email and click the embedded link to confirm your registration.
The My Account page appears, with the Email Address, Password, and Re-type Password fields pre-populated with the information you provided.
6.
Complete the remaining personal information fields. An asterisk (*) beside a field indicates that the information is required.
7.
Do one of the following to identify the name of the organization with which you are affiliated:
•
In the Affiliate Organization field, type the name of the organization and click Submit Registration.
- or -
•
If you want to search for, or add, an organization, click Search, and then follow the instructions in Searching For Trials.
8.
Click Submit Registration.
