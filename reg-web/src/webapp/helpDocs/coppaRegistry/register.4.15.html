Searching for Principal Investigators
If you are unsure of the name of the principal investigator for the trial that you are registering, you can search for one in the system and select the correct one from a list of search results.
1.
On the navigation pane, click Add a Trial, and then navigate to the Lead Organization/Principle Investigator section.
2.
Next to the Principle Investigator field, click Look Up.
The Principal Investigator Lookup page appears.
3.
Provide as much information as you can about your investigator, and then click Search.
The Principal Investigator Search Results page displays the results of your search.
If your principal investigator is not listed, you may have searched too narrowly (i.e. you may have provided too much information about the person). If the list of results is very long and contains many names that are similar to yours, you can narrow your search by providing more information. Refer to Step 5 for instructions.
4.
Scroll through the results list until you locate your principal investigator, and then click Select. The investigator’s name you selected appears in the Principal Investigator field in the Lead Organization/Principle Investigator section.
5.
If your investigator was not listed, proceed to the next step.
6.
To modify your search criteria, click Modify Lookup Name.
The Principal Investigator Lookup page appears.
7.
Do one of the following to modify your search:
•
To broaden your search so that more names are listed in the search results, delete one or more of your criteria.
- or -
•
To narrow your search so that fewer names are listed in the search results, provide more about your investigator.
8.
Click Search.
The Organization Lookup page displays the results of your new search.
9.
Repeat Step 4. If you don’t find your organization in the system, you can register it as a new one. For instructions, see Adding Principal Investigators.
