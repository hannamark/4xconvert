Changing Your Password
If you log in to the CTRP Registration Site with caGrid credentials, you can change your password on the My Account page after logging in to the application.
Remember that the new password you create will apply to all your other caGrid-enabled accounts.
If you log in with NIH/NCI credentials, you can use the NCI Password Station to change your password.
How to Change Your CTRP Registration Site/caGrid Password
1.
Log in to the CTRP Registration Site and accept the public reporting burden notice.
2.
Click My Account.
The My Account page displays your login information.
3.
Select or enter the appropriate information in the text fields and drop-down lists. Fields are described in the following table.
Field Label
Description/Instructions
Old Password
Type the password you used to log in to the application.
New Password
Type your new password
Passwords must have the following characteristics:
•
A minimum of 10, and fewer than 20 characters
•
At least one upper case letter, one lower case letter, one digit, and one non-alphanumeric symbol
•
No dictionary words
The password you create here will apply to all other caGrid-enabled accounts you may have.
Retype New Password
Type your new password again. This ensures that you have typed your password correctly the first time.
Submit
Click to save your new password.
How to Change Your NCI/NIH Password
Navigate to the NCI Password Station at http://password.nci.nih.gov. Once you have logged in to the NCI Password Station, use the Change Password feature to create a new password.
