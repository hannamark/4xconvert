Completing and Deleting Saved Drafts
During the course of registering a trial, you can save the information you have already provided as a draft, and return to it later. Once you retrieve your draft, you can complete the registration or delete the draft altogether.
If you do not complete the registration, the system will automatically delete the draft 30 days after you have saved it.
How to Complete or Delete Saved Drafts
1.
Retrieve the draft by searching for it using the Search Saved Drafts option. (For details, see Searching For Trials.)
Saved draft records are listed in the Saved Draft Search Results table.
2.
Do one of the following in the appropriate Action columns on the right side of the table:
•
To delete the record, click Delete. The draft record is deleted from the system permanently.
- or -
•
To complete the record, click Complete.
The Register Trial page displays all the information you provided previously. You can complete the rest of the information by following the steps in Registering Non-Proprietary Trials or Registering Proprietary Trials.
