Completing the Sponsor Section
You must complete all fields in the Sponsor section.
How to Complete the Sponsor Section:
1.
For clinical trials involving an agent or device or other intervention only, look up the Sponsor and select the appropriate sponsor organization from the list of search results. If your trial’s sponsor is not listed, you can register it in the system at this point. To search for and register a sponsor, follow the instructions in Searching for Registered Organizations and Adding Organizations. For Inter-Group trials, type the Lead Organization’s trial number.
2.
Indicate the party who is responsible for the trial. Select one of the following options:
•
PI (principal investigator)
- or-
•
Sponsor
