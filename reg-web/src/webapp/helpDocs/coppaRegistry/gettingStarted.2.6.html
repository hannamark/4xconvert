Updating Current CTRP Registration Site Accounts
Application support staff will create caGrid account usernames and random passwords for all current CTRP Registration Site users who do not have caGrid or NIH accounts.
Application support staff will email this new account information to you automatically; there is no need for you to contact them.
How to Update Your Current CTRP Registration SiteAccount
1.
Navigate to the CTRP Registration Site home page at:
http://trials.nci.nih.gov/registration
2.
Log in using the account name and password that you received from the Application support staff. See Logging In to the CTRP Registration Site.
3.
On the navigation pane, click My Account.
The My Account page appears, pre-populated with information from your original account, but with a new caGrid username.
4.
Complete the rest of the fields by following the instructions in Creating New CTRP Registration Site Accounts, beginning with Step 6.
