Using CTRP Trial Registration Templates and Resources
The CTRP provides templates and other resources on their website that you can use to facilitate trial information gathering, registration, update, and amendment. Additionally, a template is available to guide you through the process of registering multiple trials at one time using the CTRP Registration Siteâ€™s batch upload feature. CTRO staff use the information you provide in these documents to abstract your trials.
Instructions, rules, valid values, and specifications for using each of the templates are provided in the template files.
Refer to the glossary of CTRP terms at http://www.cancer.gov/clinicaltrials/ctrp/page12, or the Glossary in this Help to help you to understand all the data elements required.
