Amending Trial Status Dates
You must complete all fields in the Status/Dates section. Valid dates for a given trial status depend on the other values you have entered, and whether those dates are actual or anticipated. Follow the rules for trial status dates provided in Completing the Trial Status/Dates Section.
Instructions for completing the Trial Status Dates section are the same for both original and amended submissions.
