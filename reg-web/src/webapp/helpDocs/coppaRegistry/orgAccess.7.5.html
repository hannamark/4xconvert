Managing Organizational Access Through Trial Ownership
As a site administrator you can grant and remove trial ownership rights to multiple users and multiple trials at the same time.
For example, you can grant ownership of one trial to three CTRP Registration Site users. Or, you can grant one user ownership of three trials.
How to Manage Trial Ownership
8.
On the navigation pane on the left side of the page, click Manage Trial Ownership. The Manage Trial Ownership page displays the names of your organization’s CTRP Registration Site users on the left side, and your organization’s submitted trials on the right.To assign ownership, select the check box in the Allow as Owner? for each user you want to assign to a trial(s).
9.
On the right side of the page, select the trial(s) to which you want to assign ownership, and then click Assign Ownership.
10.
To revoke ownership, clear the check box in the Allow as Owner? for each user you want to unassign as an owner.
11.
On the right side of the page, select the trial(s) to which you want to unassign ownership, and then click Unassign Ownership.
