Editing Trial Details
You can edit trial details after you have reviewed them at any time before you submit the trial to the CTRP. If you want to edit a trial that you own, follow the instructions in Updating Trials.
How to Edit Trial Details
1.
Scroll to the bottom of the Register Trial page, and then click Review Trial.
2.
Scroll to the bottom of the Review Trial Details page, and then click Edit.
The Register Trial page displays all information you have provided in editable form.
3.
Make changes as necessary, and then click Review Trial.
4.
After you have reviewed your edits, click Submit.
