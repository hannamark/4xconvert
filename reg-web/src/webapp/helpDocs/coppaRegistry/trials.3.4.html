Working with Search Results
After you search for trials, a list of search results and their associated trial details appears at the bottom of the Search Trials page. You can navigate through the search results in several ways, as detailed in the following table.

Additional Notes
Sort your results by field
Click the column heading.
Click Title to sort your results by the trial title.
By default, results are sorted by NCI Identifier.
Move to the next page of results
Click Next or click the next page number above or below the list of results.
If you are on page 2 of the results and want to move to page 3, click Next.
The Next link is not active on the last page of results.
Move to the previous page of results
Click Prev or click the preceding page number above or below the list of results.
If you are on page 4 of the results and want to move to page 3, click Prev.
The Prev link is not active on the first page of results.
Move to a specific page of results
Click the specific page number above or below the list of results.
If you are on page 2 of the results and want to move to page 5, click 5.
None
Move to the first page of results
Click First above or below the list of results.
If you are on page 5 of the results and want to move to page 1, click First.
The First link is not active on the first page of results.
Move to the last page of results
Click Last above or below the list of results.
If you are on page 2 of the results and want to move to the last page, click Last.
The Last link is not active on the last page of results.
View details for a trial
Click the NCI Identifier for the trial of interest. The Trial Details page appears.
If you want to view a trial with an NCI Identifier of NCI-2007-0231, click NCI-2007-0231. The Trial Details page for the trial labeled NCI-2007-0231 appears.
1.
As a trial submitter, you can view your trials that have the status “Submitted” as well as others’ trials that have not been rejected during the validation process.
2.
Other registered users can view only trials that do not have a status of “Submitted” or “Rejected.”
Download trial documents
Click the name of the trial document.
If you want to view a trial document named Analysis.doc, click Analysis.doc.
3.
Whether or not you can view a trial document depends on the trial status and who submitted the trial details. See the note in View details for a trial above.
