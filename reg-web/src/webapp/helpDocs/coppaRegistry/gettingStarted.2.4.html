Creating and Updating CTRP Registration Site Accounts
To search for and submit individual clinical trial protocol details, you must have an account in the NCI Clinical Trials Reporting Program Trial Registration Site. You can create an account using the application’s account creation feature.
The way in which you create an account in the CTRP Registration Site depends on whether you have a current NIH or caGrid account, and whether you are a current or new CTRP Registration Site, users as follows:
•
New users who do not have a caGrid or NIH account request caGrid credentials via the CTRP Registration Site.
•
Current users who do not have a caGrid or NIH account receive a username and temporary password for a caGrid account.
•
New users who have a caGrid or NIH account use their current credentials to register for an account via the CTRP Registration Site.
