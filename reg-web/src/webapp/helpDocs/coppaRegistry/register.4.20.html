Completing the Trial Related Documents Section for Proprietary Trials
You must include a proprietary template if you did not provided an NCT number in the Trial Identification section. Currently you are required to supply your documents as Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx, or .docm), Adobe PDF, Microsoft Excel (.xls, .xlsx, .xlsm, or .xlsb), and/or WordPerfect files.
See Using the Proprietary Trial Template for template instructions and download details.
The procedure for uploading documents is the same for all document types.
How to Submit Trial Related Documents
1.
Next to the Proprietary Template field, click Browse.
2.
Navigate to, and select, the appropriate document, and then click Open.
Depending on your operating system, you may see a different command name for “Open.”
3.
Repeat the steps above for any other document you want to submit.
