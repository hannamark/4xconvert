Navigating the Validation Workspace
Submitted trials that you select for validation appear on the Trial Validation workspace with the navigation pane on the left side of the page.
How to Navigate the Validation Page
1.
Review the individual elements of the submitted trial by clicking through the links on the navigation pane
2.
When you have reviewed the submitted information, in the navigation pane, click Trial Validation.
