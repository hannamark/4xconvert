Reviewing Recorded Trial Details
Before you begin your review of submitted trial data, open or print the protocol document or its replacement so that you can compare trial details in the protocol to those displayed in Protocol Abstraction.
How to Review Trial Details
1.
In the navigation pane, click Trial Validation.
The Trial Validation page appearsdisplaying the Trial Summary on the top of the page and the Trial Details section below it.
2.
Compare the trial details in the protocol document or its replacement with those displayed on the Trial Validation page.
3.
Modify the trial details as necessary.
4.
Click Save.
5.
Search for duplicate trials to ensure that the trial you are validating is unique.
Search for the following criteria:
•
combination of lead organization and lead organization trial identifier
•
principal investigator
•
title and trial search
Note: Ignore trials that have been rejected, indicated by the processing status “Rejected.”
6.
If you found a duplicate trial, reject it by following the instructions in Accepting and Rejecting Trials.
7.
If you did not find a duplicate trial, validate other trial data as necessary, and then accept the trial by following the instructions in Accepting and Rejecting Trials.
