Abstracting Human Subject Safety Information
The information you are required to provide depends on the Board approval status and other indicators, as noted in the instructions that follow.
You will need the IRB approval document for a single site to abstract this category of information. The only field that you are required to complete in this section is the Board Approval Status. Other fields are either optional or non-editable. The latter are indicated by gray fields.
Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
How to Abstract Human Subject Safety Information
1.
On the navigation pane, click Human Subject Safety.
The Human Subject Safety Information section appears.
2.
Select or enter the appropriate information in the text fields and drop-down lists. Fields are described in the following table. Items marked with an asterisk (*) are required fields.
Field Label
Description/Instructions

Board Approval Number
If the trial has been submitted and approved, type the number assigned to the protocol by the human subjects review board in the field. If the number does not appear in the IRB approval document, enter the date. See Selecting and Entering Dates.
Board Name
Click Look Up to search for and select the Board name. If the name is not listed, register it with the CTRO. See Looking Up Registered Organizations.
When you select the Board name, the contact information is displayed automatically in the fields provided.
If you registered a new organization with the CTRO in the previous step, the system will notify you when the record is added so that you may continue your abstraction.
If applicable, complete any of the contact fields that are missing information.
Board Affiliation
If the status is other than Submission Not Required, type the name of the affiliated organization.
3.
To save the details you have abstracted, click Save.
