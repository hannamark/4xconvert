Linking Organizations to a Trial
You can associate, or link, participating sites with the trial you are abstracting.
How to Link Organizations to a Trial:
1.
On the navigation pane, click Participating Sites.
The Participating Sites Page appears.
2.
Click Add.
The Participating Sites page displays the Participating Sites tab by default. The current trial status appears on the bottom of the tab..
3.
On the Participating Sites tab, next to the Organization Name field, click Look Up and follow the instructions in Looking Up Registered Organizations.
The City, Country, and Zip/Postal Codes fields are populated with the organization information you selected.
4.
In the Site Recruitment Status field, select the status from the drop-down list.
5.
In the Site Recruitment Date field, type the recruitment date (as mm/dd/yyyy), or, click the calendar icon () and select the date from the calendar.
6.
If the lead organization or participating site is a cancer center, in the Target Accrual Number field, type the accrual number.
7.
Click Save.
The organization is linked to the trial.
