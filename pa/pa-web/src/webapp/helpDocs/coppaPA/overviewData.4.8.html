Viewing and Adding Milestones
Because milestones indicate the progress of a trail, you can view, but not change recorded milestones. However, you can add records throughout the life cycle of the trial submission.
How to View and Add Milestones
1.
On the navigation pane, click Trial Milestones.
The Trial Milestones page appears and displays all milestones recorded to date.
2.
To record a milestone, click Add.
The Add Milestone page appears.
3.
In the Milestone field, select the milestone you want to add to record from the drop-down list. The following table lists valid milestones and their definitions.

Initial Abstraction Verified Date
Date the abstractor verified the abstracted data upon approval from the principal investigator or trial submitter
Abstracted
User, if the submitter’s approval is received within 5 business days after the system has sent the TSR, or, if the approval is not received within 5 business days after the system has sent the TSR. Equal to the TSR Sent Date + 6 business days if the most current processing status is Abstracted.
Recording this milestone manually triggers the following:
•
Abstraction validation
•
Updating the trial record Verification Date to the date this milestone is recorded
•
Addition of Abstraction Verified - Response processing status if the most current processing status is Abstracted and you have received the submitter's TSR feedback
•
Addition of Abstraction Verified - No Response processing status if the most current processing status is Abstracted and you have not received the submitter's TSR feedback within 8 business days after sending it to the submitter
You cannot record this milestone if any of the following is true:
•
Abstraction validation fails
•
One or move active On-hold records exist

Abstraction-Verified (Response, No response)

•

4.
In the Date field type the date, or click the calendar icon, and select the milestone date.
5.
In the Comments field, type the action that triggered the milestone, or any comments about the event.
6.
Click Save.
The milestone is displayed on the Trial Milestones page.
7.
Repeat the steps above after you complete each abstraction update as appropriate.
