Deleting Trial-Related Documents
Protocol Abstraction enables you to delete current trial related documents. Although you can not directly delete a protocol or IRB approval document, you can replace them. See Editing and Replacing Documents
How to Delete Trial-Related Documents
1.
On the navigation pane, click Trial Related Documents.
The Trial Related Documents page appears.
Click the Delete icon () in the Delete column.The Trial Related Document page provides a text box in which you must state the reason for deleting the document, for example, “Duplicate document.”
2.
Click Done.
