Reviewing Funding Details
Ensure that the details in existing records are accurate and that there are no duplicate records.
How to Review Trial Funding Details
1.
In the navigation pane, click Trial Funding.
The Trial Funding page appears and may display one or more funding records.
2.
Ensure that there are no duplicate NIH grant records associated with the trial. If a duplicate exists, you may contact the trial submitter to request verification that NIH grants were recorded correctly.
a.
If the submitter determines that one of the records is a duplicate, delete the duplicate by following instructions in the next step.
3.
Do one of the following to continue:
•
To add funding mechanism information if no records are displayed, click Add and follow the instructions provided in Abstracting Trial Funding Information.
•
To modify an existing record, click the Edit icon () and follow the instructions provided in Abstracting Trial Funding Information.
•
To delete a record, click the Delete icon () in the Delete column.
There may be more than one record in the table. Be sure you select the appropriate Edit or Delete icon.
4.
Proceed to Reviewing IND/IDE Details.
