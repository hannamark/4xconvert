Validating Funding Details
For non-proprietary trials, ensure that the details in existing records are accurate and that there are no duplicate records.
Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
How to Review Trial Funding Details
1.
In the navigation pane, click Trial Funding.
2.
The Trial Funding page appears and may display one or more funding records.Ensure that no duplicate NIH grant records were registered. If a duplicate exists, use the protocol document to verify which record is correct. If you are unable to determine the correct record, you may contact the trial submitter to request verification.
a.
If the submitter determines that one of the records is a duplicate, delete the duplicate by following instructions in the next step.
3.
Do one of the following to continue:
•
To add funding mechanism information if no records are displayed, click Add and follow the instructions provided in Abstracting Trial Funding Information for the fields displayed.
•
To modify an existing record, click the Edit icon () and follow the instructions provided in Abstracting Trial Funding Information.
•
To delete a record, click the Delete icon () in the Delete column.
There may be more than one record in the table. Be sure you select the appropriate Edit or Delete icon.
4.
Proceed to Validating IND/IDE Details.
