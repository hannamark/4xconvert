Checking In and Checking Out Trials
Protocol Abstraction’s Check In/Check Out feature allows you to lock trials you are going to abstract to prevent other abstractors from editing the same trial at the same time. This ensures that no two abstractors can work on the same trial simultaneously. This feature also enables you to search for trials that you have checked out previously.
Remember to check out a trial before making any modification to the trial data.
A Protocol Abstraction support team member granted special privileges can check in/out any trial in the system, including one you have checked out currently.
Except for Protocol Abstraction support team members, once you have checked out a trial, other abstractors can see, but not modify, the trial.
How to Check In/Check Out Trials
1.
Search for and select a trial you want to check in (unlock) or check out (lock).
2.
In the lower section of the Trial Identification page, do one of the following:
•
To lock a trial, from the Trial Check-In/Check-Out Status drop-down list, select Check Out.
- or -
•
To unlock a trial, from the Trial Check-In/Check-Out Status drop-down list, select Check In.
3.
Click Save.
Your username is displayed at the top of the page next to Checked Out by.
The Trial Check-In/Check-Out Status drop-down list is available only if the trial you are validating/abstracting is one of the following:
•
Currently checked in for everyone
•
A trial you have checked out or checked in previously
