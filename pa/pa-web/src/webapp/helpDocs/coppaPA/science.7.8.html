Deleting Outcome Measures
You can delete existing outcome measure records during abstraction. However, each trial must have one primary and one secondary outcome measure.
How to Delete Outcome Measures
1.
On the navigation pane, click Outcome Measures.
The Interventional Trial Design â€“ Outcome Measures page displays one or more outcome measure records.
2.
In the Delete column for the record you want to delete, click the Delete icon ().
There may be more than one record in the table. Be sure you select the Delete icon in the correct row. There is no way to recover a deleted record. If you delete a record accidently, follow the instructions in Adding Outcome Measures for adding it back.
