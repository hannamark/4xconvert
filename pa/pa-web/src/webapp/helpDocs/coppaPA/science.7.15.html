Deleting Eligibility Criteria
Although you cannot delete required fields, you can delete other criteria records that may have been added during abstraction.
Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
How to Delete Eligibility Criteria
1.
On the navigation pane, click Eligibility Criteria.
The Eligibility Criteria page appears.
2.
In the Delete column for the record you want to delete, click the Delete icon ().
There may be more than one record in the table. Be sure you select the Delete icon in the correct row. There is no way to recover a deleted record. If you delete a record accidently, follow the instructions in Adding Other Eligibility Criteria for adding it back.
