Abstracting Lead Organizations and Principle Investigators
 
How to Abstract Lead Organizations and Principle Investigators
1.
In the Lead Organization field, click Look Up, and search for the organization as per the instructions in Looking Up Registered Organizations.
2.
In the Principle Investigator field, click Look Up, and search for the person as per the instructions in Looking Up Registered Persons.
