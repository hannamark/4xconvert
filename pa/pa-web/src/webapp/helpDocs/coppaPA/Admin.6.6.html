Abstracting Lead Organizations and Principal Investigators
Protocol Abstraction enables you to search for registered organizations and principal investigators.
Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
How to Abstract Lead Organizations and Principal Investigators
1.
Next to the Lead Organization field, click Look Up, and search for the organization as per the instructions in Looking Up Registered Organizations.
2.
In the Principal Investigator field, click Look Up, and search for the person as per the instructions in Looking Up Registered Persons.
