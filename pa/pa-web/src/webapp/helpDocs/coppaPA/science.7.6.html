Adding Outcome Measures
You can add multiple primary and secondary outcome measures to a trial, but you must provide at least one of each.
How to Add Outcome Measures
1.
On the navigation pane, click Outcome Measures.
The Interventional Trial Design – Outcome Measures page appears, with or without any outcome measure records displayed.
2.
Click Add.
The Add/Edit Outcome Measure page appears.
3.
Do one of the following to indicate whether the outcome measure is primary or secondary:
•
To add a primary outcome measure, in the Primary field, select Yes.
- or -
•
To add a secondary outcome measure, in the Primary field, select No.
4.
In the Outcome Measure Description field, type a description of an outcome measure. For example, “Progression-free survival” or “Frequency and severity of adverse events.”
5.
In the Time Frame field, type the duration of the outcome measure.
6.
If the outcome measure presented a safety issue, in the Safety Issue field, select Yes. Otherwise, select No.
7.
Click Save.
