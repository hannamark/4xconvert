Abstracting Central Contacts
A central contact may be a specific person, or it may be a generic title or role rather than a person. For example, the central contact may be “clinical study office,” “director of the clinical study department,” etc.
How to Abstract Central Contacts
1.
In the Central Contact field, click Look Up, and search for the person as per the instructions in Looking Up Registered Persons.
2.
If the contact is generic, follow the instructions in Abstracting Sponsors and Responsible Parties.
3.
In the Email Address field, type the contact’s main email address.
4.
In the Phone Number field, type the contact’s main contact phone number.
