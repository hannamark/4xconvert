Abstracting Central Contacts
A central contact may be a specific person, or it may be a generic title or role rather than a person. For example, the central contact may be “clinical study office,” “director of the clinical study department,” etc.
Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
Note:
Central contact information is not required.
How to Abstract Central Contacts
1.
Do one of the following to add central or generic contact information:
•
Next to the Central Contact field, click Look Up, and search for the person as per the instructions in Looking Up Registered Persons.
- or -
•
If the contact is generic, follow the instructions in Abstracting Sponsors and Responsible Parties.
2.
To remove a contact currently listed in the Central Contact field, click Remove.
3.
If you provided a generic contact, in the Email Address field, type the contact’s main email address.
4.
In the Phone Number field, type the contact’s main contact phone number.
