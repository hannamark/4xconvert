Validating Regulatory Information
Regulatory Information validation does not pertain to proprietary trials. Instructions are provided for non-proprietary trials that require XML documents for submission to ClinicalTrials.gov.
Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
How to Validate Regulatory Information
1.
In the navigation pane, click Regulatory Information.
The Regulatory Information page appears
2.
Follow the instructions provided in Abstracting Regulatory Information.
