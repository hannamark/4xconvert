Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
How to Validate Trial Details
1.
In the navigation pane, click Trial Validation.
2.
The Trial Validation page appears.Type the appropriate information in the text fields, or select options from the drop-down lists as appropriate according to the detailed instructions provided for each of the following sections of the page:
•
Validating the Trial Identifiers Section
•
Validating the Other Identifiers Section
•
Validating the Lead Organization/Principal Investigator Section
•
Validating the Sponsor/Responsible Party Section
•
Validating the Summary 4 Information Section
3.
Do one of the following to continue:
•
To save the trial information so that you can continue to validate the trial at a later time, click Save.
- or -
•
To accept or reject the trial, follow the instructions in Accepting and Rejecting Trials.
