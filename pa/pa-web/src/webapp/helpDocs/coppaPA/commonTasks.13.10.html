Accessing Trial Summary Reports
Trial Summary Reports (TSRs) contain all trial details as submitted by users via the NCI CTRP Registration Site, and is updated with the information you abstract. You can download the reports as necessary throughout the validation and abstraction processes.
Ηοω το Access Trial Summary Reports
Do one of the following to access a TSR:
•
In a trial record, for example, in a list of search results, click the View TSR link in the View TSR column. The link will be functional only after the trial has been abstracted.
The TSR is displayed in a new browser window.
- or -
•
On the navigation pane, click View TSR.
The TSR is displayed in Adobe Reader.
If you do not have Adobe Reader installed on your computer, you can download it for free from the Adobe Reader website at http://get.adobe.com/reader/?promoid=BUIGO.
