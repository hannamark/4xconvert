Managing Trial Ownership
Trial ownership allows an NCI CTRP Registration Site user to update and amend trials and to view trial details. In your role as abstractor, you can assign or revoke ownership on a per- trial basis.
Site administrators of non-proprietary trials can grant and revoke administrative rights to other users in their organization via Site Administration menus in the NCI CTRP Registration Site application. Proprietary trial submitters do not have access to the organizational access feature. They request ownership via the CTRO.
How to Assign Trial Ownership
1.
Search for the trial to which access has been requested. For detailed instructions, see Searching for Trials.
2.
Click the NCI Trial Identifier link.
3.
The Trial Identification page appears with the navigation pane displayed.On the navigation pane, in the Trial Overview section, click Assign Ownership.
4.
The Assign Ownership page appears.Type the name(s) and or email address of the user who has requested ownership, and then click Search.
5.
The Assign Ownership page displays the search result(s).In the Action column, click Assign Ownership for the appropriate user.
