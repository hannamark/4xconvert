Unlinking Participating Sites From a Trial
If a participating site currently linked to the trial you are abstracting is incorrect, you can unlink the site.
How to Unlink a Participating Site From a Trial
1.
On the navigation pane, click Participating Sites.
The Participating Sites Page appears.
2.
To disassociate the participating site from the trial, click the Unlink icon () in the Unlink column, and then confirm the action in the pop-up window.
There may be more than one record in the table. Be sure you select the Unlink icon in the same row as the record you want to unlink.
