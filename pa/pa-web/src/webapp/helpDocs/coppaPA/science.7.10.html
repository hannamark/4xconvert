Adding Other Eligibility Criteria
If the trial that you are abstracting uses other criteria in addition to the health, gender, and age of study participants, you can create additional eligibility criteria records in Protocol Abstraction.
Protocol Abstraction provides two methods for abstracting eligibility criteria, as follows:
•
Unstructured. Enables you to record eligibility criteria in free-form text. See Adding Eligibility Criteria in Free-Form Text.
•
Structured. Enables you to build eligibility criteria based on measurable values and Cancer Data Standards Registry and Repository (caDSR)-defined eligibility criteria. See Building Eligibility Criteria.
