Managing Trial Ownership
Trial ownership allows an NCI CTRP Registration Site user to update and amend trials and to view trial details. In your role as abstractor, you can assign or revoke ownership on a per-trial basis.
Site administrators of complete trials can grant and revoke administrative rights to other users in their organization via Site Administration menus in the NCI CTRP Registration Site application. Abbreviated trial submitters can not use this feature for industry-lead trials. They request ownership via the CTRO.
How to Assign Trial Ownership
1.
Search for the trial to which access has been requested. For detailed instructions, see Searching for Trials.
2.
Click the NCI Trial Identifier link.
3.
Check out the trial. See Checking In and Checking Out Trials.
4.
On the navigation pane, in the Trial Overview section, click Assign Ownership.
The Assign Ownership page appears.
5.
Type the name(s) and or email address of the user who has requested ownership, and then click Search.
The Assign Ownership page displays the search result(s).
6.
In the Action column, click Assign Ownership for the appropriate user.
The Action column indicates that trial ownership has been assigned.
