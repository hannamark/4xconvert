Abstracting Trial Title Details
 
How to Abstract Trial Title Details
1.
Trial submitters are required to provide a Lead Organization Trial Identifier. If the number is not correct, in the Lead Organization Trial Identifier field, type the unique protocol ID assigned to the protocol by the lead (or sponsoring) organization.
2.
Optionally, in the Acronym field, type the abbreviation (initial letters) by which the trial is known.
3.
In the Brief Title field, type a title that summarizes the purpose of the trial.
The PDQ staff my have provided the brief title.
4.
In the Official Title field, ensure that the title is accurate and complete (as it appears in the protocol document). Make any necessary changes or additions.
5.
In the Trial Description section, type a summary description of the trial in the Brief Summary field.
The PDQ staff my have provided the brief summary.
6.
Optionally, in the Detailed Description field, type all the information that accurately describes the trial in detail.
7.
Optionally, in the Keywords field, type a word or series of words that can help to classify the trial.
The PDQ staff my have provided keywords.
