Abstracting Participating Site Information
Trials are conducted in participating sites. You must provide information about the site itself (organization), the investigator(s), and primary contacts. When you add a participating site to a trial, you associate, or link, an organization, site, investigator(s), and primary site contact information to it.
Information on participating sites can be included in the protocol document or in the Participating Sites document.
