Processing Updated Trials and Requests for Admin Rights
Trial submitters can update trials any time after the CTRO has accepted their trials. As a result, the subset of updated trials that require re-abstraction are listed in the Protocol Abstraction Inbox. Once you have abstracted the updated information, remove the trial record from the Inbox to indicate that you have completed the updated abstraction. See Accessing Trials in the Inbox.
The Inbox also lists administrative access requests from NCI CTRP Registration Site users. See Granting Admin Rights to NCI CTRP Registration Site Users.
Trial updates do not affect any elements that are reflected in the protocol document. Changes to the protocol document that require IRB approval are submitted via trial amendments.
