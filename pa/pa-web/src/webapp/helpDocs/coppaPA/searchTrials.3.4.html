Viewing Trial Identification Details
Do one of the following to display trial details:
•
In the Search Results table, click the NCI Trial Identifier link for the trial of interest.
- or -
•
Navigate to the clinical trial you want to abstract, and click any link in the Action column. For example, navigate to the clinical trial you want to validate, and click the Validate link.
The Trial Identification page displays information about the trial as it was registered in the Protocol Abstraction system.
The top of the Trial Identification page contains a summary of the pertinent aspects of the trial, including the following information:
•
NCI/NCT Identifier
•
Official title
•
Lead organization Trial ID
•
Lead organization
•
Principal investigator’s name – link used for accessing principal investigator's contact information
•
Trial submitter’s email address – link used for accessing submitter contact information
•
Current trial status
•
Processing status
•
Amendment Number (when applicable)
•
Amendment Date (when applicable)
The summary remains visible at the top of each page as you progress through the trial abstraction.
To view the principal investigator’s or trial submitter’s contact information, click the appropriate link in the summary. A pop-up window displays the information.
The bottom half of the Trial Identification page displays most of the same information as the summary, including the following:
•
NCI trial identifier
•
Lead organization Trial ID
•
Lead organization
•
Proprietary Trial (true/false)
•
Official title
•
Trial Check-in/Check-Out Status (see Checking In and Checking Out Trials)
The combination of these elements uniquely defines the trial in the CTRP.
