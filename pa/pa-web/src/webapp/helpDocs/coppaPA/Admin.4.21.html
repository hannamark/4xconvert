Abstracting Collaborator Information
Collaborators information can be included in the protocol document or in the uploaded Participating Sites document.
You can associate, or link, collaborating organizations with the trial you are abstracting. To unlink a previously linked collaborator, see Unlinking Collaborators From a Trial.
How to Link Collaborators to a Trial
1.
On the navigation sidebar, click Collaborators.
The Collaborators page appears.
2.
Click Add.
The Collaborator page displays the fields for the organization data.
3.
Next to the Organization Name field, click Look Up and follow the instructions in Looking Up Registered Organizations.
The City, Country, and Zip/Postal Codes fields are populated with the organization information you selected.
4.
From the Functional Role drop-down list, select the collaboratorâ€™s role in the trial.
5.
Click Save.
The collaborator is linked to the trial.
