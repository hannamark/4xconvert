Deleting Arms
When you delete arms from a trial, you delete them from the system.
Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
How to Delete Arms
1.
On the navigation pane, click Arm.
The Arms page appears.
2.
In the Delete column for the appropriate record, click the Delete icon ().
Note:
There may be more than one record in the table. Be sure you select the Delete icon in the correct row. There is no way to recover a deleted record. If you delete a record accidently, follow the instructions in AddÂ­ing Arms to a Trial for adding it back.
