Abstracting Interventional Trial Outcome Measures
Trials can have multiple outcome measures. When abstracting outcome measure information, ensure that each trial has at least one primary and one secondary outcome measure. The primary and secondary measures are displayed in the Protocol Abstraction interface as “Yes” or “No” respectively in the “Primary” column.
