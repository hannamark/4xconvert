Completing Abstractions
1. Only accepted trials can be abstracted. Rejected trials cannot be abstracted.
2. All mandatory elements on listed screen must be assigned to not-NULL values.
2.1. NCI specific Information: all mandatory elements must be not NULL.
2.2. General Trail Details: all elements marked as mandatory must be not-NULL.
2.3. Regulatory Information-> Regulatory: all elements marked as mandatory and becoming mandatory under specific condition must be not-NULL.
2.4. Regulatory Information-> Human Subject Safety Information: all elements marked as mandatory and becoming mandatory under specific condition must be not NULL.
2.5. Regulatory Information-> IND/IDE: all elements marked as mandatory and becoming mandatory under specific condition must be not NULL.
2.6. Trial Status: all elements on screen and corresponding recruitments status must be not-NULL.
2.7. Interventional Trial Design -> Trial Design: all elements marked as mandatory and becoming mandatory under specific condition must be not NULL.
2.8. Observational Study Design -> Study Design: all elements marked as mandatory and becoming mandatory under specific condition must be not NULL.
3. Trial Related Documents: single active Protocol Document and single active IRB Approval document records must exist.
4. Participating Sites -> Treating Sites: trial must include at least one 'active' participating site playing treating site role.
5. Participating Sites -> Treating Sites: not completed participating site must include at least one 'active' investigator.
6. Participating Sites -> Treating Sites: each participating site playing treating site role must have 'active' primary contact info (person, phone, email) for not-completed study.
7. Disease/Condition: trial must include at least one disease/condition.
8. Interventions: Interventional trial must have at least one intervention.
9. Interventional Trial Design -> Outcomes: interventional trial must include at least one primary/secondary outcome measure.
