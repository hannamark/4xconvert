How to Abstract Regulatory Information
1.
Under the Regulatory Information section of the navigation pane, click Regulatory Information.
The Regulatory Information page appears.
2.
In the Trial Oversight Authority Country field, select the name of the country in which the oversight authority organization is located.
The oversight authorities registered in the country you selected are listed in the Trial Oversight Authority Organization Name field.
3.
In the Trial Oversight Authority Organization Name field, select the name of the organization that oversees the trial from the drop-down list.
4.
Indicate whether the trial is regulated by the FDA by selecting Yes or No in the FDA Regulated Intervention Indicator drop-down list. If you selected Yes, proceed to the next step. Otherwise, skip to Step 6.
Note:
You must select “YES” in the FDA Regulated Intervention Indicator field if the trial includes an IND or IDE.
If the trial is interventional and you indicated that it is regulated by the FDA, the Regulatory Information section displays fields for Section 801. You are required to complete these fields.
5.
Indicate whether the FDA-regulated interventional trial is an applicable trial as defined in US Public Law 110-85, Title VIII, Section 801 by selecting Yes or No in the Section 801 Indicator drop-down list.
If you indicated that the trial is applicable under Section 801, you are required to complete the Delayed Posting field.
6.
Indicate whether the release of trial information on ClinicalTrials.gov is being delayed until after an interventional device has been approved or cleared by selecting Yes or No in the Delayed Posting Indicator drop-down list.
7.
Optionally, indicate whether a data monitoring committee has been appointed for this trial by selecting Yes or No in the Data Monitoring Committee Appointed Indicator drop-down list.
8.
To save the details you have abstracted, click Save.
9.
Do one of the following to continue:
•
To review the data you provided on the previous page, click Back.
- or -
•
To proceed with the abstraction and move to the next page, click Next.
