Abstracting Trial Outcome Measures
Outcome measure information pertains to complete trials only.Trials can have multiple outcome measures. When abstracting outcome measure information, ensure that each trial has at least one primary and one secondary outcome measure. The primary and secondary measures are displayed in the Protocol Abstraction interface as True or Falserespectively in the Primary column
