Removing Trials From the Inbox
After you have abstracted an updated trial, the trial record is listed in the Inbox with an option for removal.
How to Remove a Trial From the Inbox
1.
After you have finished abstracting one or more updated trials, on the navigation pane, click Inbox.
2.
The Search Trial page appears.Type one or more characters in the NCI Trial Identifier field, and click Submit.
3.
All updated trials matching your search criteria are listed in the Search Results section.In the Remove column for the trial record you have abstracted, click Remove.
