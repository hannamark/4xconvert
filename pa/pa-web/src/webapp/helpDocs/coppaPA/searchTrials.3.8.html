Listing Updated Trials in the Inbox
You can review or abstract any updated trial record listed in the Inbox. Once listed, you can sort the updated trial records by clicking the column headings. You can not sort by the on-hold reasons, on-hold dates, or update reason columns.
Remember to remove the trial from the Inbox once you have completed the abstraction. See Removing Trials From the Inbox
How to List Updated Trials in the Inbox
1.
On the top section of the navigation pane, click Inbox.
The Search Trial page appears.
2.
Leave the NCI Trial Identifier field blank, and click Submit.
All updated trials are listed in the Search Results section.
3.
To abstract a trial, in the Action column for the trial record you want to abstract, click Abstract.
In order to initiate abstraction of a trial in the Inbox, you must lock the trial. See Checking In and Checking Out Trials
4.
To remove a trial record, in the Remove column for the trial record you have abstracted, click Remove.
