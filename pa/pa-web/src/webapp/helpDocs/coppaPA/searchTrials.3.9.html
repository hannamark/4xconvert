Searching for Trials in the Inbox
You can review or abstract any updated trial record listed in the Inbox.
Remember to remove the trial from the Inbox once you have completed the abstraction. See Removing Trials From the Inbox
How to Search for Trials in the Inbox
1.
On the top section of the navigation pane, click Inbox.
The Search Trial page appears.
2.
In the NCI Trial Identifier field, type the appropriate ID, and then click Submit.
The trial record you searched for is displayed in the Search Results section.
3.
To abstract a trial, in the Action column for the trial record you want to abstract, click Abstract.
4.
To remove a trial record, in the Remove column for the trial record you have abstracted, click Remove.
