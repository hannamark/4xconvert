Trial Summary Report Life Cycle
Trial Summary Reports (TSRs) keep submitters and/or principal investigators informed about the progress of their trials throughout the processing life cycle. They contain all the submitted, abstracted, and amended data elements in the system to date.
The TSRs are generated by the system upon the document specialistâ€™s request via the trial search functionality, the View TSR menu on the navigation pane, and via abstraction validation. They are also generated and emailed to the submitter when the Trial Summary Report Sent Date milestone is recorded. TSR feedback from the submitter or principal investigator triggers milestone events in the system. The table below shows the relationships between processing life cycle, milestone events, and TSRs.
