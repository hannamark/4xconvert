Abstracting General Trial Details
General trial details include the following elements:
•
NCI trial identifier
•
NCT trial ID
•
Lead organization Trial ID
•
Trial title
•
Description
•
Lead organization/principal investigator
•
Sponsor/Responsible party
•
Central contact
Unless otherwise noted, you are required to complete all information on the General Trial Details page.
You cannot type information directly into gray fields. Instead, use the Lookup buttons to add/edit information.
How to Abstract General Trial Details
1.
On the navigation pane, click General Trial Details.
The General Trials Details page appears.
2.
Optionally, in the NCT Number field, type the trial identifier assigned by the Clinical Trial program.
3.
Trial submitters are required to provide a Lead Organization Trial Identifier. If the number is not correct, in the Lead Organization Trial Identifier field, type the unique protocol ID assigned to the protocol by the lead (or sponsoring) organization.
