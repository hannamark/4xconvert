Deleting Interventions from a Trial
When you delete interventions from a trial, you do not delete them from the system.
Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
How to Delete Interventions
1.
On the navigation pane, click Intervention.
2.
The Intervention page appears.In the Delete column for the appropriate record, click the Delete icon ().
Note:
There may be more than one record in the table. Be sure you select the Delete icon in the correct row. There is no way to recover an deleted record. If you delete a record accidently, follow the instructions in AddÂ­ing Interventions to a Trial for adding it back.
