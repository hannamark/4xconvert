Validating Participating Sites
Participating Sites validation pertains only to abbreviated trials. Ensure that the following information is complete and accurate:
•
Organization Name
•
Local Trial Identifier
•
Site Principal Investigator
•
Site Recruitment Status
•
Site Recruitment Status Date
•
Date Opened for Accrual
•
Date Closed for Accrual
Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
How to Link Organizations to a Trial:
1.
On the navigation pane, click Participating Sites.
The Participating Sites Page appears.
2.
Do one of the following to continue:
•
To add participating sites, click Add and follow the instructions provided in Abstracting Participating Site Information.
•
To modify an existing record, click the Edit icon and follow the instructions provided in Abstracting Participating Site Information.
•
To delete a record, click the Delete icon.
3.
If you made any changes, click Save.
