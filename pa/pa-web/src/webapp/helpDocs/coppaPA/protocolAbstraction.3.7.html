Abstracting NCI-Specific Information
Unless otherwise noted, you are required to complete all information on the NCI Specific Information page.
How to Abstract NCI Specific Information
1.
On the navigation sidebar, click NCI Specific Information.
The NCI Specific Information page appears.
2.
In the Reporting Data Set Method field, select the method by which the principle investigator reports accrual. Table 3.3The following table lists valid methods.

In the Summary 4 Funding Category field, select the type of external sponsor or funding source based on the role/responsibility/participation in the study. The following tableTable 3.4 lists valid categories:

In-house, internally reviewed trials, including those collaborative studies conducted with industry sponsorship in which the center is a primary contributor to the design, implementation, and monitoring of the trial, or participation in a multi-site trial initiated by an investigator at another center.

4.
In the Summary 4 Funding Source field, look up the Summary 4 Funding Sponsor/Source and select the appropriate organization from the list of search results. If your trial’s funding organization is not listed, you can register it in the system at this point. To search for and register an organization, follow the instructions in Chapter 5, Working With Organization Records,.
5.
To save the details you have abstracted, click Save.
6.
Do one of the following to continue:
