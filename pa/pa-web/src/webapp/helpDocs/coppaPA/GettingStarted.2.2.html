Overview of NCI Clinical Protocol Abstraction Portal
Abstraction is the identification and extraction of data elements from submitted trial-related documents by the NCI Clinical Trials Reporting Office (CTRO) staff. The NCI Clinical Protocol Abstraction Portal (Protocol Abstraction) enables authorized users to to query, access, and validate submitted protocols; and to abstract accepted protocols through a common user interface that ensures standardization of data across clinical trials.
