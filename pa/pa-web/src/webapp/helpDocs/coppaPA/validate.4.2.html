About Trial Validation
The objective of trial validation is to ensure that the clinical trial data submitted via the NCI Clinical Trials Reporting Program Registration Site are accurate according to the protocol and/or other trial-related documents. During the validation process you can make corrections to submitted information as you review each protocol element.
If you determine that the submitted data is correct and complete, accept the trial. Otherwise, reject it. See Accepting and Rejecting Trials
