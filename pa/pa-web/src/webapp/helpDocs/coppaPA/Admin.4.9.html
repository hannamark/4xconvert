Abstracting NCI-Specific Information
Unless otherwise noted, you are required to complete all information on the NCI Specific Information page.
How to Abstract NCI Specific Information
1.
On the navigation sidebar, click NCI Specific Information.
The NCI Specific Information page appears.
2.
In the Reporting Data Set Method field, select the method used for the principle investigator summary report. The following table lists valid methods.
Method
Definition
Abbreviated
Requires minimal subset of data for reporting
(for example, demographics)
Complete
Larger set of data (for example, includes outcomes)
AE (Adverse Events)
Adverse events statistics are reported.
3.
In the Summary 4 Funding Category field, select the type of external sponsor or funding source based on the role/responsibility/participation in the study. The following table lists valid categories:
Funding
Category
Definition (For clinical trials involving an agent or device or other intervention)
National
National Cooperative Group Trials
Externally
Peer-Reviewed
R01s and P01s or other trial mechanisms funded by NIH or supported by other peer-reviewed funding organizations.
Institutional
In-house, internally reviewed trials, including those collaborative studies conducted with industry sponsorship in which the center is a primary contributor to the design, implementation, and monitoring of the trial, or participation in a multi-site trial initiated by an investigator at another center.
Industrial
Design and implementation of the study is controlled by the pharmaceutical company
4.
In the Summary 4 Funding Source field, look up the Summary 4 Funding Sponsor/Source and select the appropriate organization from the list of search results. To search for an organization, follow the instructions in Looking Up Registered Organizations.
5.
To save the details you have abstracted, click Save.
Summary 4 information is required, as is the lead organization or at least one participating site that is a NCI-designated cancer center.
6.
Do one of the following to continue:
•
To review the data you provided on the previous page, click Back.
- or -
•
To proceed with the abstraction and move to the next page, click Next.
