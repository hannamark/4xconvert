Abstracting On-hold Information
On-hold records contain information about a delay in trial processing that includes the cause of the delay, the date the delay started, and the date that the trial was taken off hold.
You can add and edit On-hold information records at any time during the validation and abstraction processes. However, the only data in on On-hold record that you can edit is the Off-hold date. That is, you can take a delayed trial off hold by editing an existing On-hold record.
A trial can have one or more On-hold records at any time for a single trial.
