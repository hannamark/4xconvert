Intervention Model
Design of an interventional study.
Intervention Model Values
Definitions
Single Group
Single arm study
Parallel
Participants are assigned to one of two or more groups in parallel for the duration of the study
Cross-over
Participants receive one of two alternative interventions during the initial phase of the study and receive the other intervention during the second phase of the study
Factorial
Two or more interventions, each alone and in combination, are evaluated in parallel against a control group
