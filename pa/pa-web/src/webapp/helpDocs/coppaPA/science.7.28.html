Deleting Sub-groups
When you delete sub-groups from a trial, you delete them from the system.
How to Delete Sub-groups
1.
On the navigation pane, click Sub-group.
The Sub-group page appears.
2.
In the Delete column for the appropriate record, click the Delete icon ().
Note:
There may be more than one record in the table. Be sure you select the Delete icon in the correct row. There is no way to recover a deleted record. If you delete a record accidently, follow the instructions in AddÂ­ing Sub-groups to a Trial for adding it back.
