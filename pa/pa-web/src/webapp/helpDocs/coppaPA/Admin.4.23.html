Abstracting Trial-Related Documents
Each trial must include the following types of documents:
•
For non-industry trials only: Protocol Document (complete protocol)
•
For industry trials only: A summary of the protocol in lieu of a Protocol Document.
•
IRB approval
•
Informed Consent (if not included in the protocol document)
Note:
If the informed consent information is not included as part of the proto­col document, you can upload Informed Consent document.
•
Participating sites
•
For multi-site trials: A list that includes the following:
•
participating sites
•
investigators
•
primary contact information
•
collaborators
•
For single-site trials: Collaborator information
If collaborators are not listed in the protocol document, they may be listed in the Participating Sites document.
Note:
If the Participating Sites information is not included as part of the proto­col document, you can upload it.
You are required to supply your documents as Microsoft Word, Adobe PDF, Microsoft Excel, and WordPerfect files.
