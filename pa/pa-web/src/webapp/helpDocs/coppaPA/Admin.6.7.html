Abstracting Sponsors and Responsible Parties
How to Abstract Sponsors and Responsible Parties
1.
In the Sponsor field
2.
Indicate whether the sponsor is a principle investigator (PI) or a sponsor by clicking one of the options.
3.
Optionally, in the Responsible Party Contact field, click Look Up, and search for the person as per the instructions in Looking Up Registered Persons.
4.
In the Email Address field, type the responsible party’s main email address.
5.
In the Phone Number field, type the responsible party’s main contact phone number.
