Validating the Lead Organization/Principal Investigator Section
 
This section pertains to both non-proprietary and proprietary trials. However the Principal Investigator information is not applicable to proprietary trials.
The Lead Organization is the institution that is the principal administrative organization responsible for the study. In multi-site trials that do not have a single assigned coordination center, every participating organization can be named as lead organization.
Review the Lead Organization and Principal Investigator submitted with the trial and make any changes as necessary. .
How to Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data. Validate the Lead Organization/Principal Investigator Section
1.
For non-proprietary trials, next to Lead Organization, click Look Up Org, and select the appropriate organization from the list of search results.
2.
Next to Principal Investigator click Look up and select the appropriate name from the list of search results.
The Principal Investigator is the person who is responsible for the study.
