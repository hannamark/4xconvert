Unlinking Collaborators From a Trial
If a collaborator currently linked to the trial you are abstracting is incorrect, you can unlink it from the trial.
How to Unlink a Collaborator From a Trial
1.
On the navigation pane, click Collaborators.
The Collaborators page appears.
2.
To disassociate the collaborator from the trial, click the Unlink icon () in the Unlink column, and then confirm the action in the pop-up window.
There may be more than one record in the table. Be sure you select the Unlink icon in the same row as the record you want to unlink.
