Abstracting Collaborator Information
Collaborator information can be included in the protocol document or in the uploaded Participating Sites document.
You can associate, or link, collaborating organizations with the trial you are abstracting. To delete a previously linked collaborator, see Deleting Collaborators From a Trial.
Before you begin abstracting or validating any trial, be sure you check it out first. Otherwise you will not be able to modify any trial data.
How to Link Collaborators to a Trial
1.
On the navigation pane, click Collaborators.
The Collaborators page appears. NCI is added as a collaborator automatically.
2.

3.
Next to the Organization Name field, click Look Up and follow the instructions in Looking Up Registered Organizations.
The City, Country, and Zip/Postal Codes fields are populated with the organization information you selected.
